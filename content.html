Environmental Health

Air Quality and Pollution Control

This subtopic addresses the sources, impacts, and mitigation strategies related to air pollution, focusing on reducing emissions from industrial, vehicular, and domestic activities to protect public health and reduce the risk of respiratory and cardiovascular diseases.

Water Quality and Access

This area explores the importance of clean water for drinking, sanitation, and hygiene, discussing challenges such as contamination, waterborne diseases, and the strategies for ensuring safe water access and sustainable water resources management.

Waste Management and Recycling

Focusing on the reduction, proper disposal, and recycling of solid and hazardous wastes, this subtopic emphasizes sustainable practices to minimize environmental impact, prevent pollution, and promote public health through effective waste management systems.

Climate Change and Health Impacts

This subtopic examines how climate change affects environmental determinants of health, such as clean air, safe drinking water, sufficient food, and secure shelter, and discusses adaptation and mitigation measures to protect communities from climate-related health risks.

It also highlights the significance of addressing mental health concerns that arise from environmental stressors, recognizing the profound impact that climate change and environmental degradation can have on psychological well-being.
